Our Story

Passion For Every Detail

Tommy Wholesale Rental is a leading wholesale rental company. We specialize in providing a wide range of rental props and accessories for the creative designer, including candlesticks, candelabras, fountains, ceremony chuppahs and arches, chairs, dance floors, and much more. Our items can be used standalone or further enhanced with flowers, fabric, greenery, and candles to create stunning event displays. We proudly serve all New England states, including Massachusetts, Connecticut, Vermont, Rhode Island, and New Hampshire.
